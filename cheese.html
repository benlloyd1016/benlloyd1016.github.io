80 Days of Cheesemaking

After learning about Welsh cheesemaking while researching my own Welsh heritage, I thought it would be fun to try out the art of crafting cheese myself!

Over 80 days, I'll work through David Asher's "The Art of Natural Cheesemaking", starting with simple yogurt cheeses and progressing to complex aged varieties.

Join me here as I go "around the wheel in 80 days"!
