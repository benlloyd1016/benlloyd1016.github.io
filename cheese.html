Learning Basic Cheesemaking

After learning about Welsh cheesemaking while researching my own Welsh heritage, I thought it would be fun to try out the art of crafting cheese myself!

I am loosely working through David Asher's "The Art of Natural Cheesemaking", starting with simple yogurt cheeses and progressing to complex aged varieties.
